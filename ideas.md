**Design Movement**: **Neo-Brutalism / Data-Industrial**

**Core Principles**:
1.  **Radical Honesty**: Expose the grid, raw data, and structure. No smoothing over the complexities of trading.
2.  **Information Density**: High-contrast, dense layouts that respect the user's ability to process data.
3.  **Precision**: Sharp edges, exact alignments, and a feeling of engineered reliability.
4.  **Speed**: Interactions should feel instantaneous and snappy, not floaty.

**Color Philosophy**:
-   **Background**: Deep, void-like black (`#050505`) or very dark gunmetal.
-   **Foreground**: Stark white (`#FFFFFF`) for primary text.
-   **Accents**:
    -   **Signal Green**: Electric, terminal green (`#00FF41`) for profit/success/go.
    -   **Alert Red**: High-vis red (`#FF3333`) for risk/stop/danger.
    -   **Data Blue**: Cold, technical blue (`#00F0FF`) for neutral data streams.
-   **Intent**: Evoke the feeling of a terminal or a heads-up display (HUD). High contrast for readability in low-light trading environments.

**Layout Paradigm**:
-   **Modular Grid**: Visible grid lines or distinct borders separating content areas.
-   **Asymmetry**: Offset headers and overlapping elements to break the monotony of standard SaaS layouts.
-   **Split Screen**: Vertical divides to contrast "Natural Language" input vs. "Code/Data" output.

**Signature Elements**:
-   **Monospace Typography**: Use a high-quality monospace font (e.g., JetBrains Mono, IBM Plex Mono) for ALL headers and data points. Sans-serif only for long-form body text.
-   **Crosshairs & Tickers**: Subtle animated crosshairs on hover; scrolling tickers of raw data.
-   **Glitch/Terminal Effects**: Very subtle text decoding animations on load.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
-   **Haptic Visuals**: Buttons depress deeply (no shadow, just displacement) or invert colors instantly on hover.
-   **Hover-Reveal**: Show detailed metrics or "under the hood" data when hovering over simplified cards.

**Animation**:
-   **Staggered Reveal**: Elements snap into place (no fade-ins).
-   **Typewriter Effect**: For the "Natural Language" inputs.
-   **Data Stream**: Constant, subtle background movement of numbers or charts.

**Typography System**:
-   **Headings**: `JetBrains Mono` (Bold/ExtraBold). Uppercase.
-   **Body**: `Inter` or `Switzer` (Regular) for readability, but kept tight.
-   **Data/Captions**: `JetBrains Mono` (Regular).
</text>

**Design Movement**: **Swiss Style / International Typographic Style (Dark Mode)**

**Core Principles**:
1.  **Objective Clarity**: Content is king. The design recedes to let the data speak.
2.  **Mathematical Grids**: Strict adherence to a hierarchical grid system.
3.  **Asymmetric Balance**: Dynamic layouts that feel stable but not centered.
4.  **Sans-Serif Purity**: Reliance on a single, versatile typeface family.

**Color Philosophy**:
-   **Palette**: Monochrome (Black, White, Greys) with a single, bold accent color (e.g., International Orange or Electric Blue).
-   **Intent**: Convey professionalism, institutional grade, and Swiss precision.

**Layout Paradigm**:
-   **Grid-Based**: Everything aligns perfectly.
-   **Negative Space**: Extensive use of black space to frame content.

**Signature Elements**:
-   **Huge Typography**: Massive, tight-leading headlines.
-   **Horizontal Rules**: Thick lines separating sections.
-   **Geometric Shapes**: Simple circles or squares to denote status or categories.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
-   **Smooth & Linear**: Transitions follow straight lines.
-   **Functional**: Hover states purely indicate interactivity (e.g., underline or color shift).

**Animation**:
-   **Parallax**: Subtle depth between text and background layers.
-   **Sliding**: Panels slide in from sides.

**Typography System**:
-   **Font**: `Helvetica Now` or `Neue Haas Grotesk` (or open source equivalent like `Inter` set tightly).
-   **Hierarchy**: Size and weight denote importance; no decorative elements.

**Design Movement**: **Cyber-Physical / Glassmorphism 2.0**

**Core Principles**:
1.  **Depth & Layering**: Using blur and transparency to show hierarchy.
2.  **Neon Glow**: Soft lighting effects to highlight active elements.
3.  **Fluidity**: Organic shapes meeting digital precision.
4.  **Immersive**: The interface feels like a window into a complex system.

**Color Philosophy**:
-   **Background**: Deep indigo/purple gradients.
-   **Accents**: Cyan, Magenta, Neon Green.
-   **Intent**: Feel futuristic, high-tech, and "AI-native".

**Layout Paradigm**:
-   **Floating Cards**: Content floats above the background.
-   **Centered Focus**: Key interactions happen in the center of the viewport.

**Signature Elements**:
-   **Glass Borders**: 1px white borders with low opacity.
-   **Background Blurs**: Behind active modals or navbars.
-   **Gradients**: Text gradients for headlines.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
-   **Glow on Hover**: Elements light up when interacted with.
-   **Magnetic Buttons**: Buttons pull towards the cursor slightly.

**Animation**:
-   **Fluid Morphs**: Shapes change form smoothly.
-   **Shimmer**: Loading states look like energy passing through.

**Typography System**:
-   **Headings**: `Sora` or `Outfit` (Modern Geometric Sans).
-   **Body**: `DM Sans`.
